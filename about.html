Primafaculty is a whole and sole website related to the faculty of XYZ College. It was established on 10th July 2020. This website manages the faculty of the respective college and provides assistance to faculty to manage their information related to their career such as their resume and other work related stuff regarding to college. It also provides information to their students about their faculty and their achievements and education. This helps to maintain the transparency between faculty and students and let the students understand their faculty at a deeper level. Primafaculty makes sure that the faculty can easily access their information and keep it updated for their students and organisation.
